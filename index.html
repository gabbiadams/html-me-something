Ride.

My husband and I moved to Northwest Arkansas in March of 2019. It's an area we visited frequently when we lived in Oklahoma. We have both been riding and racing bikes for a pretty long time, and the infrastructure for bikes and all other outdoor activities is pretty amazing in this area. We live in the middle of the Back 40 trail system, and spend lots of time riding awesome trail and scenic gravel roads. Here are just a few of my favorite areas to check out on the bike:

Bella Vista trails
Slaughter Pen trails
Coler Mountain Preserve
Gravel riding in NW Arkansas & SW Missouri
